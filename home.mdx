# What is Verne<sup>®</sup>?

**Verne® is the Cloud-based, Registry Aware® solution for governments that is the easiest and fastest way to implement flexible, tailored online statutory registries. Verne® comes with all the capabilities to build and support a registry, meaning you focus your energies on new and improved functionality using configuration options rather than through code changes.**

# Get the best from Verne<sup>®</sup>

**For consultants, configurators, testers or registry administrators, the Verne® Learning & Support Hub is the place to find what you need to get the best from your Verne® installation.**
